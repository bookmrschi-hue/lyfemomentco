My Story
Built from Love,
Fueled by Craft

I'm a first-generation American, born of African parents, raised right here in the Inland Empire. I grew up watching my family pour love into every celebration — and that love never left me.

By day I'm a substitute teacher across the Brea Olinda, Corona-Norco, Fontana, Ontario-Montclair, Moreno Valley, and San Bernardino school districts. I've been in classrooms with your kids. I know what these graduation moments mean to families.

I fell in love with crafting in high school — making scrapbooks for friends, handcrafting cards, designing keepsakes that nobody else had. Over time, those scrapbooks became digital designs, and those digital designs became a full business.

I spent 10 years in digital marketing for events, leading festival and event marketing projects that taught me the power of a beautiful, well-executed experience. Then I asked myself: why can't families in my community have that same level of quality?

So Lyfe Moment Co. was born. I believe in great work, beautiful execution, and fair prices — not cheap prices, because this work has real value, but prices that honor the families I grew up with.

I'm the Queen of DIY, and I want to help every family who loves personalization and themed experiences feel like they have a professional in their corner — whether they come to me, or I give them the tools to do it themselves.
