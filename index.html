Before You Order
Policies & FAQs ✦
How many revisions are included?
1 revision only. You must respond to your digital proof within 24 hours or printing begins with no further changes allowed.
When will my order ship?
Orders ship 4–7 business days after proof approval. Plan your event date accordingly and include it at checkout.
Do you offer rush orders?
Yes! Rush orders are available — contact us before purchasing. Rush fees apply and vary by order size and timeline.
Can I return or cancel my order?
No returns or refunds on personalized or custom items. No cancellations once the design process has begun.
What's the difference between a treat bag and a party favor kit?
Treat bag = custom-printed bag only (no contents). Party favor kit = fully assembled kit with tissue holder, hand sanitizer or lip balm, chocolate mints, and custom name topper.
Do beverage labels include drinks?
No — beverage labels are printed labels only. Drinks are not included. Local candy delivery is available in the Corona, CA area only.
Will colors look exactly like on screen?
Colors may vary slightly from your screen due to monitor calibration and print processes. We match as closely as possible.
How do I pay?
We accept Stripe (card via Buy Now buttons), Zelle, and Cash App ($LyfeMomentCo). Event planning and grad packages are invoiced after consultation.
